How it works

01📷

Capture

A camera mounted on the glasses frame continuously captures the user's field of view in real time.

02🧠

Analyze

The VisionCraze Neural Engine processes each frame, identifying obstacles, terrain, individuals, and hazards.

03🔊

Narrate

A speaker delivers clear audio alerts such as "obstacle two feet ahead" or "stairs going down, eight steps."

04🧭

Navigate

With continuous environmental awareness, users navigate any setting safely and independently.
